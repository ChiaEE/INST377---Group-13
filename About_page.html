This is a website that's designed to help movie directors in making film plots. This website gives users the ability to search the database by the title of the book and ensures that the books are public Domain by allowing users to filter books based on copyright claims. This website uses Project Gutenberg's Gutendex API to perform searches and extract information on books.

How to Navigate this website?

This website has a couple features on it that aid in navigation - The menu up top, and the homepage's search button.

Search Bar

Type in keywords like 'Adventure' or 'Dracula' to search for similar titles and click to search. To randomize your experience, simply type in a letter. Like the letter 'M' to see different kinds of books You may stumble upon one you like. But be sure to check its copyright status! You may have to be a bit patient, we have so many books and it takes a while for the page to load!

Copyright Dropdown

Select the dropdown options to filter books based on copyright status

Popular Books Chart

To explore the genres listed in the Navigation Bar, simply click on the Genre dropdown button to reveal a selection of available genres. Once you've chosen a genre of interest, click the 'show chart' button to unveil a visually informative bar graph showcasing the most downloaded books within that particular genre. Each bar represents a distinct book, and by hovering over individual bars, you can access specific download counts for each book, providing valuable insights into their popularity and relevance within the chosen genre.
Again, we have alot of book info! You may have to click twice for the chart to appear!

Book Carousel

To read excerpts or full versions of the books, scroll down to the carousel at the bottom of the page and select one of the options from the random selections displayed. The carousel showcases a variety of books, each offering a preview or the complete text for you to enjoy. The selection of books changes every time the page is refreshed, ensuring a unique browsing experience each visit. This dynamic feature is designed to help you discover new and interesting reads you might not have encountered otherwise. If a book catches your eye, be sure to click on it quickly to explore further, as it may not be available in the next set of random selections.
